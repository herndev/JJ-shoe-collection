Hiking Shoes

Hiking (walking) boots are footwear specifically designed for protecting the feet and ankles during outdoor walking activities such as hiking and provide comfort.

More Detail

  • Stable

    The shoes' heel support / heel cup should be soft and stable, preventing vertical or horizontal heel movement.

  • Max 2.5cm (1 inch) heel

    The shoe's heel must not exceed 2.5cm in height. Otherwise, the ankle become more unstable to sprains and forefoot pain.

  • In good condition

    Monitor your footwear and discard shoes that are too worn-down, as these have often lost some of their basic functions.
